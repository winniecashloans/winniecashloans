Frequently Asked Questions
How quickly will I receive my loan? ▼
Once your application is approved via WhatsApp, funds are typically transferred within the same day. Speed depends on the time of application and verification.
What do I need to apply? ▼
You'll need a valid ID, proof of income or employment, and an active South African bank account. All details will be confirmed during the WhatsApp application process.
What is the maximum loan amount? ▼
Loan amounts are assessed on a case-by-case basis. Contact us via WhatsApp to discuss the amount you need and we'll advise on eligibility.
Can I repay early? ▼
Yes! Early repayment is encouraged. The interest rate applied is fixed at either 50% (≤15 days) or 60% (>15 days) based on your agreed repayment period.
What happens if I miss my repayment date? ▼
Additional daily interest will accumulate from the day after the due date until the full balance is paid. It is critical to repay by 10:00 AM on your agreed date.
How do I make a repayment? ▼
Repayment instructions will be provided during your application process via WhatsApp. Always confirm your payment has been received before 10:00 AM on the due date.
